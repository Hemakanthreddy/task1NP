"NayePankh Foundation" is a non governmental organisation with a strong desire to help the society and make it a better place for all, by doing everything in our power and to make our vision successful we would require your vital support. Service to mankind is the service to god. Let’s revolutionise the society together!.

Our Mission

To provide support and assistance to underprivileged individuals and communities, empowering them to overcome challenges and improve their quality of life through various initiatives and programs.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities for personal growth, fostering a society that is inclusive, compassionate, and sustainable.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We believe in showing empathy and kindness towards those in need.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all our actions.
  • Collaboration: We value partnerships and teamwork to maximize our impact.
  • Empowerment: We strive to empower individuals and communities to become self-reliant.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and new approaches to address social challenges effectively.
